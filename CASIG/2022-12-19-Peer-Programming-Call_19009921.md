# Climate Action SIG : 2022-12-19 Peer Programming Call

Created by Si Chen, last modified on Dec 21, 2022

**Summary**

We end the year on a high note!  [Bertrand WILLIAMSRIOUX](https://lf-hyperledger.atlassian.net/wiki/people/712020:57613290-3ad4-4f53-8166-19a9bdb9c047?ref=confluence) and I got a demo working together which shows:

- Tokenizing emissions for a natural gas utility from fugitive methane (Scope 1) and downstream (Scope 3) emissions
- Tokenizing emissions for an electric tuility from combustion and operations (Scope 1) and purchased emissions (Scope 2)
- Transferring emissions from natural gas and electric utilities to a building based on natural gas usage in therms and electricity usage in KWH
- Transferring emissions from building owner to building tenant based on square feet of leased space.
