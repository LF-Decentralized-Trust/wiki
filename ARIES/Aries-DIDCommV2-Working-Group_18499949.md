# Hyperledger Aries : Aries DIDCommV2 Working Group

Created by Lance Byrd, last modified by Stephen Curran on Nov 23, 2022

Community to discuss and improve the adoption of DIDComm v2 across Aries implementations (agents, mediators, wallets, etc) and beyond. 

Not sure what DIDCommV2 is? Check out [DIDComm.org](https://didcomm.org/) to get started. 

Meetings are held weekly at 15:00 in Central Europe / 6:00 Pacific
